                Hi! I'm a teenage photographer based in Romania and on this website you can find my collection of favourite photos. I've been doing photography for 3 years and my favourite styles are street photography,
                nature photography and portrait photography. Recently I've won a contest with the theme nature in the city, and since then I started to gain confidence in my work.
                That's why I decided to upload some of my best shots on shutterstock and adobe stock, so the world can find out about me.
                
                My gear consists in an Olympus OM-D E-M10 Mark II camera, the 14-45mm f3.5 kit lens, 25mm f1.7 prime Lumix G lens and 100-300mm f4.0 Lumix G Vario lens.
                Recently I started film photography with a Canon EOS 3 and currently I'm trying to fix an old full manual russian camera (Zorki 4).

                If you want to support me, you can check out my work on shutterstock, adobe stock and my instagram page. You can find them at the Support me and Contact sections from the menu.
